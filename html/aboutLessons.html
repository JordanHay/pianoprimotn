Performance Opportunities

Piano students are invited to participate in the Knoxville Federation Festival and the Knoxville Music Teacher's Association's auditions for critique and awards. As social distancing allows, students enjoy a private Christmas recital/party and a public spring recital. Advanced students are provided with opportunities to earn scholarship money and auditions for the Governor's School for the Arts.

Knox County Lessons

Knoxville area residents have the opportunity to take lessons from Jeannine Hines at Lane Music where she rents a piano studio. Lane Music is located at 9648 Kingston Pike in Franklin Square, convenient to I40 and Pellissippi Parkway. You can learn more about Lane Music at their website.
