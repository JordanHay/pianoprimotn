I teach students to play the piano at my home in Maryville, online or at Lane Music in Knoxville. I have students who perform at Federation Festival auditions, Knoxville Music Teachers auditions and in various competitions. I am certified by the Music Teachers National Association. I enjoy teaching students how to read music and enjoy playing it!
I take all precautions available during this time and disinfect the piano keys, music rack, pencils and doorknobs between lessons. All students will also be required to wear a mask. Lessons start at 25 dollars per half hour.
