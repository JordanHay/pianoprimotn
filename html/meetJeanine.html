Jeannine Hines has been a piano teacher for over 40 years. She grew up in Indiana where she attended Huntington College, then Taylor University where she earned a Bachelor's degree in music education. After leaving Indiana she lived and taught piano for 2 years in Michigan and 17 years in South Carolina. She has lived and taught in East Tennessee for 18 years. Jeannine enjoys teaching students of all ages, from 4 to 84+! She has earned certification from the Music Teacher's National Association.
